Burma Biodiversity Platform

Connecting Nature, Data, and People

Welcome

The Burma Biodiversity Platform is your gateway to exploring the rich biodiversity of Burma through open data, species records, and community knowledge. We bring together the power of a public portal and an information system to serve nature lovers, researchers, and citizen scientists alike.

Projects

  • Burma Birds – Explore bird species in Burma
  • Burma Biodiversity Portal – Browse species and ecosystems
  • Coming soon: Marine & Freshwater Biodiversity Portals

Our Mission

To build a collaborative and open biodiversity information system for Burma that supports education, conservation, and research through accessible and verifiable data.
